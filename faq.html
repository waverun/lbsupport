FAQ - LessonBlazer

1. Reporting a Subject

You can report a subject directly from the main screen by selecting the subject and using the "Report" option. Note that reporting a subject does not automatically remove it. If you wish to remove a reported subject, you can manually remove it from the main screen.

2. Course Quota

Each course you add or create in the search page deducts one quota from your account. When you sign up, you receive a free quota balance. The LessonBlazer team reserves the right to adjust the amount of free quota at any time.

3. Purchased Courses

When you purchase a course, it is recorded under your logged-in user account. If you add a previously purchased course after removing it, or on another device using the same account, no quota will be deducted.

4. Buying Additional Quota

You can purchase additional quota by using the "Buy Quota" option available in the user menu on the main screen. The LessonBlazer team reserves the right to adjust prices and quota amounts for purchases at any time.
